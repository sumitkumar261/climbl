Our Services

Internship and Placement season can be a difficult and emotional time, with repeated rejections causing many to feel overwhelmed or even depressed. It's important to stay positive and not let negative thoughts take over. Seeking help can also make a big difference. At Climbl, we offer guidance sessions to help you stay on track and keep your preparation in line with what employers are looking for. With our support, you can overcome the challenges of the internship search and achieve your career goals.

Personalized Feeback and Customized Plans

Our program provides individualized feedback and personalized plans to help you achieve your desired goals, with an emphasis on skill improvement and internship acquisition. Our dedicated mentors offer continuous support to ensure your success. Let us assist you in maximizing your potential.

One-on-One Internship/Placement Guidance

Our one-on-one internship guidance offers personalized support for your career search, with mentors providing tailored advice, assistance with challenges and application navigation. Our commitment is to empower you with the necessary tools and knowledge to achieve success in on-campus or off-campus internship and placement opportunities.

Regular Meet Ups for Progress Updates

Our regular meet up service offers a convenient way for team members to share progress updates and stay on track with their goals. By scheduling these meetings on a consistent basis, we help ensure that everyone is aligned and moving towards success.

Project Ideas to Work on

Our mentors will provide project ideas for you to work on, which will help you build your skills and prepare your resume.

Additional Resources

We are dedicated to providing you with the additional resources you need to succeed. With our extensive collection of resources and expert guidance, we can help you achieve your goals and take your project to the next level. Contact us today to learn more about how we can support you.

Available 16 hours a day, ready to serve your needs

Our service is available 16 hours a day to provide high-quality support and assistance to our customers. Contact us whenever you need us, and our team of professionals will be there to help you achieve your goals.
